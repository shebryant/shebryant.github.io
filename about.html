I want to build software that enchants my inner child, gains the respect of my very serious developer peers, and makes the lives of the people who use it better. I am a maker of things, lover of big ideas, doubt defier, perpetual dreamer, but doer of things that must be done. A teacher, turned fundraiser, finally turned developer--because life is too short not to do what enthralls you--my aim now is to create clean code that transforms into meaningful and beautiful things.

I recently graduated from Dev Bootcamp where I built cool things mostly with Ruby, Javascript, Sinatra, Rails and even some with Swift 2.0, but the single greatest skill I gained was how to teach myself new things, quickly! I've learned that there isn't anything I cannot learn.

As a budding developer always up for a challenge, I embrace Agile methodology, and with lots of experience pair programming, I approach work collaboratively and with a team orientation. I undertake projects one vertical slice at a time, and maintain tight feedback loops. While the continual honing of my technical skills is critically important, I value--just as much--the ability to work with empathy alongside others and strive to be a strong cultural asset for any company for which I work.

I possess both the passion and curiosity of a young developer with a keen interest in new technologies, and the business acumen of a seasoned professional with 10 years of pre-industry work experience and transferable skills.
