About me

I want to build software that enchants my inner child, gains the respect of my very serious developer peers, and makes the lives of the people who use it better. I am a maker of things, lover of big ideas, doubt defier, perpetual dreamer, but doer of things that must be done. A teacher, turned fundraiser, finally turned developer--because life is too short not to do what enthralls you--my aim now is to create clean code that transforms into meaningful and beautiful things.

I recently graduated from Dev Bootcamp where I built cool things mostly with Ruby, Javascript, Sinatra, Rails and even some with Swift 2.0. DBC was an exhilirating and exhausting experience at once, an experience for which I will always be grateful. Now that it's over, I'm ready to build bigger, better things. With Y-O-U.
